A few strengths of mine that I have noticed about myself are that I am self motivated, a critical thinker and a natural born leader.

I would describe myself as being self motivated because of my ambitious nature to achive success and fulfilment. Critical Thinker

In terms of critical thinking I would say its a strength of mine because I am objective individual so I rely heavily of factual information. I try to develop this aspect of myself by gathering information then applying it to a problem or situation in order to come to a logical conclusion.

Leadership

Leadership is another strength I display as I always like to encourage and inspire persons so that they can put their best foot forward in order to achieve the common goal that was set. I always try to improve this area of myself by assuming leadership roles in any given situation whether in clubs, socities,sports and even academic groups.
